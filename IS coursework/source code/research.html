Our Landing page

Our Landing Page VS Inspired Landing Page

Creating the index page for any website is a crucial task, and finding the right balance between simplicity and attractiveness is key. Despite our extensive research, generating ideas proved challenging initially. Fortunately, inspiration struck when we stumbled upon an image on the internet, providing valuable insights into an effective index page design. We've implemented this inspiration, ensuring our index page is straightforward and user-friendly. Notably, we've included a login form on the index page to prioritize the confidentiality of customer information. We express our gratitude to the creator of the image that sparked our ideas, as it significantly contributed to the design of our website. Our approach involves collecting only essential personal data required to enhance our services and improve user experiences.

Our Product Page

Our product page and Daraz product page

After successfully creating our index and about us pages, we encountered a roadblock when it came to designing our product page. Initially, we struggled to come up with ideas. However, one day, inspiration struck when we looked at Daraz, Nepal's leading e-commerce platform. We examined Daraz's website code through inspecting elements and sought guidance on YouTube to enhance our understanding of creating an effective product page. Through persistent hard work and dedication, we finally managed to craft the product page for our website. I want to express sincere gratitude to the YouTuber and platforms like Daraz, as they played a crucial role in providing the inspiration and insights we needed to bring our product page to life..
